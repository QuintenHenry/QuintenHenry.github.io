Multiplayer Looter Shooter

This is a project I made in Unreal Engine in my free time. I worked on this by myself, therefore there isn't much art in the game since almost all of it was free art from the store, or found online. My goal was to learn a lot about networking and to create a fun game. I made an FPS shooter with a lot of different weapons and abilities. It also has a building mechanic. You can find loot in the houses, gather wood from trees, build your house, fight your friends, drive a car, team up against bosses, ...

There is also a dynamic day and night cycle. In the night, monsters appear and you have to fight them off with your friends or by yourself. The game has over 30 weapons, some more rare than others, ranging from a boomerang to an AWP. I had a lot of fun creating this project and learned a lot. Me and my friends also had a lot of fun playing this game.
