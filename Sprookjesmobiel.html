De SprookjesMobiel

A primary school asked us to develop a game based on a fairytale. Together with my team we made De SprookjesMobiel, a game based on "De Goede Waterput" for the children of that school. We often did playtests with the children and applied their feedback. The end result is a game which can be played on both Ipad and Windows. Both the school and the children were very happy with the endresult.

We worked on this game with 3 programmers and 3 artists
