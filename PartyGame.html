Party Game using Unity,SignalR and ASP.net

This game is still a WIP. I've been working on this game for a few months in my free time and plan to publish it one day. I've had a ton of playtests already and it was always a lot of fun.

Players connect to the game by using their phone and going to the game's website where you can join the room. You can then control your player using the controls on the website. These controls range from regular buttons to drawing an object, shaking your device, using your device as a steering wheel, etc. There is a wide variety of minigames and also a boardgame to tie everything together.

This is a solo project, however I am planning on working with an artist in the future.
